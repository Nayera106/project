FOoDY.

~~~INGREDIANS~~~

  • 1 cup bright white candy melts
  • 1–2 tablespoons vegetable shortening or coconut oil
  • Red Sprinkles
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • 7 large frozen strawberries
  • 2 cup strawberry ice cream

~~~HOW TO MAKE~~~

  1. Melt candy melts in microwave according to package direction.
  2. Stir in shortening or coconut oil ~1 teaspoon at a time until it is runny enough to slowly drip off spoon.
  3. Transfer to a ziplock bag and snip one corner off to give a 1/4-inch opening. Pipe the white chocolate onto the milkshake glass rim in varying thickness so it runs down the glass. Add sprinkles.
  4. Transfer to the fridge immediately to harden, at least 10 minutes and up to 2-3 days.
